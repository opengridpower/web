OpenGrid Prototype in Rwanda Jan 13, 2025

The first OpenGrid prototype, expected to be completed in Rwanda in March, 2025!

The first distributed DC grid prototype, expected to be completed in Rwanda in March, 2025! It will provide electricity for 10 families. The capacity will provide power supply to each family for:

  • 5 LED lights
  • 1 TV
  • 1 Radio
  • Mobile phone charging

Max power is about 300W.
