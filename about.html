About OPENGRID

OpenGrid Power is an open intelligent microgrid

Our History

It was initiated and founded by Sherry Liao in 2024.

Its main features include philanthropy, an open system, and an open-source architecture.

Our Mission

To make electricity accessible and beneficial to all of humanity.

It aims to establish a low-cost, easy-to-maintain, and affordable power system for over 1 billion low-income people in underdeveloped regions worldwide.

Electricity has been invented for over 100 years, and it should, like clean air, fresh air, and warm sunlight, improve the quality of life for all humanity.
